Multimedia Group

Multimedia Group (MMG) is led by Dr. Jing Liu and affiliated with School of Electrical and Information Engineering, Tianjin University. Our goal is to train computers to enhance and understand the multimedia. Equipped with deep learning models, machines can improve the perceptual quality of digital multimeda content and accurately identify and classify objects that people are interested in, and then react to what they “see".

多媒体组

多媒体组由刘婧领导，隶属于天津大学电气自动化与信息工程学院。我们的目标是训练计算机来增强和理解多媒体。机器配备了深度学习模型，可以提高数字多媒体内容的感知质量，准确识别和分类人们感兴趣的对象，然后对他们“看到”的东西做出反应。
